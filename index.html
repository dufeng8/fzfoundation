About Us

FengZheng Cultural Foundation (FZCF) established in 2012, is a Texas-registered charity organization with IRS 501(c)3 tax exempt status. The mission of FZCF is to promote education equality and enhance education access for students and diverse families throughout the world. In order to fulfill our mission, under discretion of the Board, we provide just-in-time grants and scholarships on annual and/or need basis, and sponsor charitable projects and organizations which benefit the underserved populations domestically and internationally.
