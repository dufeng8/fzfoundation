Our Mission

Currently, we are sponsoring charitable projects and organizations that benefit migrant children and their families. In China, the population of migrant children (0-17) has reached 35.81 million. Among them, 2.05 million school-age children cannot attend public schools. Migrant schools serve these children's need for basic education. There is a shortage of resources,teachers, and systematic support in these migrant schools. The migrant children’s educational needs can’t be met sufficiently. They need to have access to quality education. Our Foundation has been supporting a few migrant schools by donating funds for educational instruments. From time to time, we will sponsor fund-raising projects for these migrant schools.
