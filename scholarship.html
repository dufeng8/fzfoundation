Application Material

Feng Wantao Scholarship, approved by the FZCF Board of Directors in 2015, supports the mission by providing merit-based scholarships to students with financial needs, in order to encourage learning and pursuing human knowledge.

Mr. Feng (1936-2015) taught middle school and high school students for over thirty years. He helped underprivileged children in Beijing after retirement. He had always wished to help diligent students from low-income families gain access to higher education. The Scholarship in remembrance of Mr. Feng Wantao will select one to five awardees from all applicants each year, based on merit and financial need. The amount of each award varies from $500 to $5000 dollars.

Applicants should submit the completed application form, along with all supporting documents by email to dufeng8@gmail.com by November 30 of the current year.
